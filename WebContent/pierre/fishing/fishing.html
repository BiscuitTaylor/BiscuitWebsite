Gone Fishing

It all began last winter, that time I was ice-fishing. Then I've been fishing many times at various places.
Here fishing while on a short canoe tour around Sikås, some small village in Jämtland.
That night, or the night before, we laid net on the lake, using this rowboat.
The amazing thing was that we caught a fish on it, the first fish of the season, a huge Pike or whatever that is. (Gädda).
