Öringfiske (trout fishing)


It was a day of warmer waters, the fish were somehow more awake, and they took the bait, both on a normal fishing pole (no fish then with the fly fishing), both trout we caught at about the same time, both the same size. Strange.



I went on to catch one more trout the next time, slightly larger and more bloody. I think the water was still to cold for Sven Martin's fly fishing.
That's the fish's blood, by the way, not mine.
